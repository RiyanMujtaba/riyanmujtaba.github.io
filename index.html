About me

18. Self-taught.
Always building.

I'm Riyan — 18 years old from Hyderabad, Pakistan. Just finished school at The City School and have been teaching myself to code and building projects ever since.

I got the chance to co-author a research paper that got accepted at CHI 2025 — still kind of surreal honestly. The paper looks at how generative AI systems can be studied and audited.

Most of what I build is stuff I actually want to use — a gym tracker, a uni search tool, an FPS game. I figure that's a good reason to build something.
